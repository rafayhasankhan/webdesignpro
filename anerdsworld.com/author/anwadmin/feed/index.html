What is Search Engine Optimization (SEO)?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of fine-tuning a website to achieve higher rankings in search results like Google.

My 4 Back-to-the-Basics SEO steps:

1. Are the keywords that you want to rank for actually on your page?
2. Are you worthy of a Google ranking?
3. Are your website users enjoying your website and content?
4. Are other websites of authority linking to you?

How to Rank your website on Google???
Simple answer is – Reverse Engineer your SEO Strategy!

1. Find Competitor(s) Domains
2. Pull Stats on SEMrush.com of each Competitor
3. Pull Competitors Keyword List
4. Understand the Keywords
5. Create your Keywords List
6. Create Website Page Guidelines (different guidelines for each keyword and page)
7. Create Website Pages (but better). One Website Page for each Keyword!
8. Submit your Domain to Free Web 2.0’s + Local Directories
9. Steal Backlinks from your Competitor
10. Watch Statistics on your Website Pages
11. Revisit Pages Regularly and Update

Successful entrepreneurs don’t wait for success to come to them – they go out and make things happen. Hustling can help you become a better entrepreneur as it helps you manage your time properly and take your own initiatives. By improving your hustle, you can take your business to the next level.

A strong work ethic is a vital part of being successful in your business. Work ethic is a set of values based on the ideals of discipline and hard work. Forming good habits such as focusing, staying motivated, honest with your clients, finishing tasks immediately, and more helps to create a good work ethic that will impress prospects and clients.

Hustling teaches you the importance of determination despite failures and roadblocks. A successful entrepreneur understands that failure, rejection, or negative feedback are not signs of stopping, but signs to keep moving forward and persevering.
